Welcome to Green One.

As of June 29, 2015, the game can be played here (hosted at Joel's previous student website)

Green One is an HTML5/JavaScript-based side-scroller built in 9 weeks by Joel Hunsaker, Aaron Schraufnagel, and Kyle Sessions, with help from Marilyn Ferguson. It was built lovingly to provide a challenge and not be beaten the first time through. It was made easier to demonstrate full playability.

Make your way across multiple worlds, using your gravity switch and laser cannon to get to the other side of the planet. Green One incorporates the Phaser.io framework, with a combination of HTML5/CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and JSON.
